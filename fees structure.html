Flexible Payment Plans

Annual Payment Plan

Pay the full year's fees upfront and receive the maximum discount benefits.

  • 5% early payment discount
  • No processing fees
  • Priority registration
  • Free financial planning session

Semester Payment Plan

Split payments into two convenient installments per academic year.

  • 2% early payment discount
  • Low processing fees
  • Flexible due dates
  • Mid-year adjustment options

Quarterly Payment Plan

Spread payments across four quarterly installments for better cash flow management.

  • Manageable quarterly payments
  • Standard processing fees
  • Automatic payment options
  • Budget-friendly approach

Monthly Payment Plan

Pay monthly for maximum flexibility and easier budget management.

  • 10 monthly payments
  • No summer payments
  • Auto-debit convenience
  • Most budget-friendly

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Available Financial Assistance

Merit-Based Scholarships

Academic excellence scholarships covering 25-75% of tuition for outstanding students with exceptional grades and achievements.

Need-Based Financial Aid

Comprehensive financial assistance program for families demonstrating financial need, covering up to 90% of total educational costs.

Talent Scholarships

Special scholarships for students with exceptional talents in arts, sports, music, or leadership, offering 15-50% fee reduction.

Sibling Discounts

Family-friendly pricing with 5% discount for families with one sibling and 10% discount for families with multiple children.

Work-Study Programs

Part-time work opportunities for students to earn while learning, reducing overall educational costs by up to $2,000 annually.

Early Bird Discounts

Special pricing for families who complete enrollment before deadline dates, offering 3-5% early registration discounts.

Accepted Payment Methods

Credit & Debit Cards

Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover cards accepted with secure processing.

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfers and wire transfers with detailed payment instructions provided.

Online Payment

Secure online portal with automatic payment scheduling and receipt generation.

Check Payment

Personal checks, cashier's checks, and money orders accepted at the business office.

Auto-Pay

Automatic payment plans with email notifications and failed payment alerts.

Financial Services

Third-party financing options and educational loan assistance programs available.
