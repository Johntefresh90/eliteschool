Application Requirements

Completed Application Form

Fill out our comprehensive online application with personal details, academic background, and extracurricular activities.

Academic Transcripts

Official transcripts from current and previous schools, showing grades from the last 3 years of study.

Recommendation Letters

Two letters of recommendation from current teachers, preferably in core academic subjects.

Personal Essay

A thoughtful essay (500-750 words) describing your goals, interests, and why you want to attend Elite Academy.

Standardized Test Scores

SAT, ACT, or other standardized test scores (if applicable for your grade level).

Personal Interview

Participate in a 30-minute interview with our admissions team, available in-person or virtually.
