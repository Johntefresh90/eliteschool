Assessment & Evaluation

Our comprehensive assessment approach ensures accurate measurement of student progress and learning outcomes

Formative Assessment

Ongoing evaluations including quizzes, projects, and class participation to monitor learning progress.

Summative Assessment

End-of-unit tests, final exams, and comprehensive projects measuring overall learning achievement.

Peer Assessment

Collaborative evaluation activities that develop critical thinking and communication skills.

Self-Assessment

Reflective practices that help students understand their own learning process and set goals.

Portfolio Assessment

Collection of student work demonstrating growth and achievement over time.

Performance Assessment

Real-world tasks and presentations that demonstrate practical application of knowledge.
