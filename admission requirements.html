Admission Requirements by Grade Level

Academic Requirements

  • Age Requirement
    Must be age-appropriate for grade level as of September 1st
  • Previous School Records
    Transcripts from previous school (if applicable)
  • Assessment Evaluation
    Age-appropriate academic and social readiness assessment

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
    Official certified copy
  • Immunization Records
    Up-to-date vaccination documentation
  • Passport-sized Photos
    2 recent color photographs
  • Parent/Guardian Information
    Complete contact and emergency information

Elementary Admission Process

Our elementary admission process focuses on developmental readiness and social-emotional preparedness for our rigorous academic environment.

Academic Standards

  • GPA Requirement
    Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA from previous school
  • Standardized Test Scores
    SSAT or equivalent standardized test results
  • English Proficiency
    TOEFL/IELTS scores for non-native English speakers
  • Teacher Recommendations
    Letters from current Math and English teachers

Assessment Requirements

  • Entrance Examination
    Mathematics, English, and Science assessment
  • Writing Sample
    Timed essay on assigned topic
  • Student Interview
    30-minute interview with admissions committee
  • Parent Interview
    Meeting with parents to discuss school philosophy

Academic Excellence Standards

  • GPA Requirement
    Minimum 3.5 cumulative GPA with strong academic trajectory
  • Standardized Test Scores
    SSAT, PSAT, or SAT scores (grade-appropriate)
  • Course Prerequisites
    Completion of required middle school courses or equivalents
  • Extracurricular Activities
    Demonstrated leadership and community involvement

Advanced Requirements

  • Academic Portfolio
    Samples of best academic work and projects
  • Personal Statement
    500-word essay on academic goals and aspirations
  • Letters of Recommendation
    3 letters from teachers, counselors, or mentors
  • Comprehensive Interview
    Extended interview covering academics, goals, and fit

Transfer Student Requirements

  • Academic Standing
    Good standing at current/previous school
  • Transcript Evaluation
    Complete academic records and credit evaluation
  • Reason for Transfer
    Written explanation for school change
  • Adjustment Plan
    Academic and social integration strategy

Mid-Year Transfer Process

  • Space Availability
    Admission subject to available openings
  • Quick Assessment
    Expedited evaluation and placement testing
  • Orientation Program
    Comprehensive introduction to Elite Academy

Application Tips

Submit your application early to ensure all materials are processed in time for review.

Request recommendation letters at least 4 weeks before the deadline.
Schedule your entrance exam and interview as soon as possible after application submission.
